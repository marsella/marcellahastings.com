Hackathon Projects

ColorMe


ColorMe is a song visualizer that lets you enter your favorite artist and see a representation of their most popular songs in color. Made at the Echo Nest Music Hack Day in November 2013.
Source code

Looper


An online game with the goal of "recreating childhood musical discovery and reckless noisemaking," created with a team at HackingArts, held in September 2013.
To play, click to draw circles, add color to the different sections, and hit the spacebar to make them play.

Tic Tac Toe


The classic tic-tac-toe game implemented using Elm, a functional reactive language. Created at the Fall 2013 Tufts Hackathon, as an exercise in using Haskell-like languages to do cool things.
To play the game, use the Elm Emulator and paste in the source code.
